It's time to LED

Energy Efficient

With the rising costs of electricity, the cost factor of electricity is becoming increasingly relevant. LED light bulbs can reduce the energy costs of lighting by more than 60%

Longer Life Span

On average LED lights can run for roughly 5 years without needing to be changed. LEDs tend to have a more durable build quality, as the lights will not shatter on impact compared with traditional lights

Save Money

Currently in Vancouver, the cost of electricity will be constantly increasing over the next 10 years. Simply changing your existing lights to LED, you can not only offset this increase but SAVE money as well.

Greener

LED lighting is the greenest solution available on today's markets. LEDs, do not contain the highly toxic mercury, which is harmful not only to the environment but also to your personal health.
